4 Reasons Why You Need A Self Storage Unit

People need storage units for all different reasons and you might be surprised to find that you need one yourself! Of course if you are moving house you will probably need to store your furniture somewhere but why else would you need one?

    Reason 1: To Reclaim Your Shed
  •         At Locked Up Storage we have many long term clients who take advantage of our low prices to free up space in their garden and reclaim their sheds! Are you fed up with all those bikes, lawn mowers, BBQs and tools making your garden look like a junk yard? Then it could be time to pop them into storage!
    Reason 2: To Store Childhood Stuff
  •         Ok so your kids left home but that doesn’t mean they won’t want their stuff back at some point! Free up their old bedroom for yourself without throwing out their childhood memories and feeling guilty! Store old toys, books, furniture and clothes in a handy unit at Locked up Storage!
    Reason 3: To Start A Small Business
  •         Whilst you can’t run your business from a storage unit you can use it to store all your equipment. Let’s say you are running a car garage and you want to store some excess parts or spares somewhere safe. Perhaps you have a business premises but you need a storeroom where you can pop in…Locked up Storage has you covered!
    Reason 4: Life Changes
  •         We all go through changes and have to start again at some point. Whether you are downsizing from a house to an apartment following a separation or need to store items following a bereavement, Locked Up Storage can provide you with an ideal unit size for your belongings. Remember that you can rent units by the month so it’s yours for just as long as you need.
